Second Saturday Project

We believe that the church should take hope and love into the world. We do this by serving the communities around us in practical ways. The second Saturday of every month we can be found somewhere in the community doing service projects at schools, parks, homes or wherever there is a need that we can meet. We also believe its a great opportunity for us to work side by side with others who may never go to a church. If you are interested in joining us on our Second Saturday Projects contact us and we would love to have you join us!
